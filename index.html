About Me

Hi, I'm Trent Hand! If I had to describe myself, I would say that I'm very hard to describe. I've spent portions of my life as a janitor, student, soldier, salesman, and an expat in Turkey. Each of these wildly divergent paths have led me to enjoy a wide variety of activities and skill sets.

Whether coding a new web app, teaching English to non-native speakers, reading a great book, cooking an elaborate (though not always well-presented) meal, or spending time chatting with friends and strangers alike, my greatest joy is learning something new.

After over a decade as a saleperson, the time felt right to move into a career which provides an opportunity to constantly grow and learn. Some friends recommended I check out coding, as it's both analytical and artistic. Turns out, it's a great fit! I can't imagine doing anything else now.
