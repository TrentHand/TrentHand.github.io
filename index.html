About Me

Energetic and positive, I'm always happy to be learning and growing in a collaborative environment. Working with a team of software developers to build apps has become an extremely rewarding experience.

I really enjoy the process of software development. Taking an idea from a vague concept into a fully fleshed-out product users can actually benefit from is an amazing feeling. I'm drawn to the deep levels of planning and organization required for any successful application to reach fulfillment.

Also, I've been a salesman for over a decade, so interacting with teammates and product owners at any level is always a great experience with me. I'm a developer who likes developing relationships(data-driven or otherwise) :).
